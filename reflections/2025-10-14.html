Reflection — 2025-10-14

Lecture 02 — Role of the Project Manager, Stakeholders, and Environments

  • The session focused on what a project manager actually does: align goals, manage trade-offs, and keep the project on track.
  • I liked the point that canceling a bad project can be the right call—stopping waste is sometimes success.
  • I learned stakeholders don’t share one definition of “success,” so expectations must be negotiated and documented.
  • New idea: treat dev vs production as different risk zones—experimentation belongs in dev, stability is mandatory in prod.
