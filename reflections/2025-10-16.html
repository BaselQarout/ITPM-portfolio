Lecture 03 — Online Video: PMBOK Knowledge Areas and Enterprise Success

  • The video refreshed the PMBOK knowledge areas and why they function like a toolkit rather than theory.
  • What stood out is that the PM’s job is integration—connecting people, plans, and priorities into one system.
  • I learned that success isn’t “deliver a thing,” it’s “deliver value that fits the organization’s strategy.”
  • New idea: stakeholder satisfaction is a performance metric, not a soft extra.
