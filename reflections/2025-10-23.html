Lecture 06 — Online Video: Waterfall Model Overview

  • The video explained Waterfall as a step-by-step SDLC where each phase gates the next one.
  • I liked the clarity of the model because it forces documentation and checkpoints.
  • I learned why it struggles with change: late requirement updates become expensive once earlier phases are locked.
  • New insight: Waterfall fits best when requirements are stable and regulation/documentation is important.
