Lecture 16 — AI in Project Management: Predictive Tools vs. Real Intelligence

  • The session continued the AI discussion and framed AI mostly as prediction rather than true understanding.
  • Even if you disagree with the “bubble” label, the key value is learning to separate capability from marketing.
  • I learned predictive tools can support estimation, forecasting, and early risk signals, but they don’t replace judgment.
  • New insight: treating AI as accountable decision-maker is a category error—projects still need human ownership.
